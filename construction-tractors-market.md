<p><p>The construction tractors market is primarily categorized into two types: crawler tractors and wheeled tractors. Crawler tractors operate on tracks, providing better stability and traction on uneven terrain, making them ideal for heavy soil and rough conditions. Conversely, wheeled tractors have tires, offering greater speed and mobility on smoother surfaces, suitable for urban environments and transport tasks. Both types are essential for various construction applications, supporting activities like grading, excavation, and earthmoving.</p></p>
