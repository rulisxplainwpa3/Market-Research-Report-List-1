<p><p>Turning chucks are essential tools in machining, categorized into manual and automatic types. Manual turning chucks require operator intervention for securing and releasing workpieces, offering precision and control. They are favored in small-scale or customized operations. Conversely, automatic turning chucks enhance efficiency by automating the clamping process, making them ideal for high-volume production environments. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs, with the manual variant focusing on flexibility and the automatic variant emphasizing speed and consistency in operations.</p></p>
