<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Metal Enclosed Capacitor Banks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LV Capacitor Banks</li><li>MV Capacitor Banks</li><li>HV Capacitor Banks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal enclosed capacitor banks are categorized into three main types based on their voltage levels: LV (Low Voltage) capacitor banks, MV (Medium Voltage) capacitor banks, and HV (High Voltage) capacitor banks. LV capacitor banks are used in applications requiring lower voltage support, typically up to 1 kV. MV capacitor banks operate within the voltage range of 1 kV to 35 kV, suitable for industrial and commercial uses. HV capacitor banks handle voltages above 35 kV, primarily for grid applications, enhancing power factor and stabilizing voltage levels.</p></p>
