<p><p>Asset Performance Management (APM) software encompasses two key market types: Asset Integrity Management and Asset Reliability Management. Asset Integrity Management focuses on ensuring that physical assets operate safely, efficiently, and within regulatory standards by monitoring their condition and performance. In contrast, Asset Reliability Management emphasizes optimizing asset performance and minimizing downtime through predictive maintenance strategies and real-time monitoring. Together, these components enhance operational efficiency, reduce risks, and extend asset lifespan, ultimately driving better decision-making and profitability for organizations.</p></p>
