<p><p>The medical compression tape market can be categorized into dynamic and static types. Dynamic compression tape allows for mobility while providing support, making it ideal for athletic use and recovery from injuries, enhancing circulation during physical activity. In contrast, static compression tape is designed for more stationary conditions, offering consistent pressure to manage swelling or support injured areas without allowing movement. Both types serve distinct purposes, catering to different patient needs and therapeutic objectives in rehabilitation and injury management.</p></p>
