<p><p>The brick machine market is divided into three main types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Automatic brick machines require minimal human intervention, producing bricks efficiently and consistently, making them ideal for large-scale production. Semi-automatic machines offer a balance, requiring some operator involvement while still enhancing productivity and reducing labor costs. Manual machines rely heavily on human labor for operation, making them suitable for small-scale or artisanal brick production, where customization and flexibility are valued over high output.</p></p>
